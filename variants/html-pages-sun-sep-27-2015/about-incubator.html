is A Place for fast growth

Kiron ventures is a Berlin based start-up incubator program, funding driven and innovative entrepreneurs that want to create social impact.

Our program bridge ideas from ideation to a ready to fund concept by providing a team with Business mentoring, Technological support, co-working space and a network of social investors, entrepreneurs, professionals and government members. Every programs offered by Kiron ventures is in partnership with other top quality incubators all over Europe which provide their expertise in an specific sector.

We have an open call for entrepreneurs to submit ideas and apply for the program but also work close together with corporations to stablish problems that they want to tackle and attract entrepreneurs to solve the challenges.

How do we support our Startups

It is all about providing the tools to be able to execute and generate a social impact.

NETWORK TO CREATE AN IMPACT

By tackling a problems affecting 50 Mio people world wide (the refugee crisis) we have gained an unique network and experience in the field of social entrepreneurship

Mentoring

Every start-up will be coach by experts, investors, the best entrepreneurs in Berlin and supported by our network of partner incubators all over Europe.

Technologycal support

We will provide the technical skills that you are missing in your team, our network of motivated designers, developers and engineers are willing to help and partner with you.

CO-WORKING SPACE

 We provide amazing environments for start-up to be creative and share ideas, we promote the creation of synergies among all the entrepreneurs at our spaces. 

mission

 “ Provide our network of impact investors and corporate partners with the best social startUps, and provide the new ventures with all the tools and skills they required to create a sustainable, scalable business ” 

vision

“ Have the biggest portfolio of social impact innovation unicorns in Europe. ”  

How do we meassure success

We focus on 3 aspects; all Kiron Ventures startups are expected to run a sustainable company that can provide a reasonable return on investment (5 to 10%). Startups must be capable to grow their business at fast paste and more important they should create massive quantifiable social impact.
