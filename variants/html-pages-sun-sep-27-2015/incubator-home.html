3 MONTHS PROGRAM

Migrationhub

Migration hub is an open physical space for ideas and partnerships to blossom. 
You can share ideas, learn about other entrepreneurs' solutions and maybe meet cofounders for your next impact project.

Address: Berlin (Potsdamer Strasse 144, 10783 Berlin).

The opening hours are extensive, but naturally not unlimited. Come take a look until December 2015!

1) Get selected

Apply, pitch and get selected to take part in the programme.

2) Benefit from the programme

Get financial support, office space, technical support, professional advice and access to a broad network (Kiron, MigrationHub and others) during a period of three months.

3) START-UP BOAT

Get an invite to the Start-Up boat, an event to create personal connections with the community and investors.

4)Mentor and investor

At the end of the program if your idea proves to have a market fit, sustainability and more importantly a social impact, an investor will support you with a small capital investment and a senior start-up will be appointed as your mentor.

5) Fund raising

Together with our partners we will continue to support you and advise you on how to apply for various financing sources.

The start-up boat

An event  designed for the integration of corporates, investors and start-ups to help to tackle social problems through the design thinking methodology.
