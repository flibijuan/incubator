Innovation & scalability

Your product or service can be a webapp, a mobile app or a physical, but it must be innovative and scalable. 

Social impact

Kiron ventures targets at startups whose project is related to a social problem. for the moment, we focus on projects to for the refugee crisis.

Team work

We only take teams of minimum 2 or 3 founders with complementary backgrounds. If you are alone, you'd better find yourself a co-founder.

long term sustainability

Even if we look for social impact as first criteria, we look for business models that can be sustainable in the long-term

work with us

We place a great deal of importance on the team, on its solidity and on its ability to be coached and to take feedback. 

proof of concept

You should have already started to work on your idea. There must be a prototype or some market research somewhere.
